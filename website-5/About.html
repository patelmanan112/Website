ABOUT US

Jo Khao, Wahi Khilao…

Redefining online food ordering & delivery where you can get the best homemade food at your door steps in no time.

Our Mission

Jo Khao, Wahi Khilao

We bring in technology & solutions for building a healthy society by delivering the best homemade food from nearby home chefs.

Your neighbourhood kitchens are going to serve what they are going to serve their family. Our basic motto is “Jo Khao Wahi Khilao”. So, you can rest assured that you are going to get healthy & tasty home food that has soul in it. In short, “Home food delivery that you count on us from the comfort of your home”.

Our Vision

“Healthy India”

Unifying India by removing unseen regional cuisine boundaries

Creating a healthy India by converting outside food equivalent to home cooked food.

Our Values

“Cooking with Love”

Empowering all home chefs to earn from the same daily cooking that they have been doing for years for their loved ones.

Health, Satisfaction, Collaboration & Happiness are our core values.

What We Offer?

Daily Order

A user can check live on our mobile app about what neighboring kitchens are preparing. You can place instant orders based on your choice and availability.

Advance Order

Having weekend plans or guests are coming home. You can place a single or bulk food order in advance (max. two days before) from your favourite kitchen.

Regional Snacks

Are you missing regional snacks such as pickles, sweets or any traditional snack from your region? Do not worry we got you covered.
